Changxin Liu – Biography

Bio

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Automation in East China University of Science and Technology, Shanghai, China. I received my PhD in mechanical engineering under the supervision of Prof. Yang Shi from the University of Victoria, Canada, in 2021. Prior to my current position, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Division of Decision and Control Systems in KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, working with Prof. Karl H. Johansson, from 2021 to 2023. I was a recipient of the DAAD-Canada One-Year Research Grants in 2020, the NSERC Postdoctoral Fellowship in 2023, and the Excellent Young Scholars Fund Program (Overseas) of the National Natural Science Foundation of China in 2023.
