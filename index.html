Short Biography

I am a postdoc researcher with the Division of Decision and Control Systems at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden, working with Prof. Karl Henrik Johansson. Before moving to Sweden, I received my Ph.D. in mechanical engineering under the supervision of Prof. Yang Shi from the University of Victoria, BC, Canada, in 2021. My research focuses on distributed optimization and control over networks.
