Bio

Dr. Changxin Liu is a Professor at the East China University of Science and Technology, Shanghai, China. He received the PhD degree from the University of Victoria, Victoria, BC, Canada, in 2021. He was a postdoctoral researcher at the Division of Decision and Control Systems in KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, from 2021 to 2024. His current research interests include decision-making in complex manufacturing systems, distributed optimization and its applications, and cyber-physical systems security.
