Bio

I am a postdoc researcher with the Division of Decision and Control Systems at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden, working with Prof. Karl H. Johansson. Before moving to Sweden, I received my Ph.D. in mechanical engineering under the supervision of Prof. Yang Shi from the University of Victoria, BC, Canada, in 2021. I was a recipient of the NSERC Postdoctoral Fellowship in 2023.

Research Interests

I am interested in the control, optimization, and learning of network systems, and their applications in cyber-physical systems and robotics. My current research focuses on the following two areas:

  • Security and Privacy in Distributed Machine Learning

  • Distributed Optimization for Control of Dynamical Systems
