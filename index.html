Short Bio

I am a postdoc researcher with the Division of Decision and Control Systems at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden, working with Prof. Karl H. Johansson. Before moving to Sweden, I received my Ph.D. in mechanical engineering under the supervision of Prof. Yang Shi from the University of Victoria, BC, Canada, in 2021.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in control theory, optimization, and machine learning, and their applications in cyber-physical systems and robotics. My current research addresses the following two areas:

  • Privacy and security in distributed machine learning

  • Distributed optimization and control
